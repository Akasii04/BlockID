Frequently Asked Questions

How is BlockID different from traditional identity systems?
Unlike traditional systems where your identity data is stored in centralized databases controlled by organizations, BlockID gives you complete ownership and control of your identity through decentralized blockchain technology. This eliminates single points of failure and puts you in charge of your personal information.
What happens if I lose my phone or device?
BlockID includes secure recovery mechanisms that allow you to restore your identity on a new device without compromising security. This is done through a combination of recovery keys, biometric verification, and multi-factor authentication protocols that ensure only you can regain access to your identity.
Is my personal data stored on the blockchain?
No, your actual personal data is not stored directly on the blockchain. Instead, the blockchain stores cryptographic proofs and pointers to your encrypted data, which is stored securely on your device and in decentralized storage. This approach maintains your privacy while leveraging blockchain's security benefits.
How do organizations verify my identity with BlockID?
Organizations can request specific identity attributes through the BlockID platform. You control what information to share and can use zero-knowledge proofs to verify information without revealing the actual data. This ensures minimal disclosure while still providing the necessary verification for services.
What devices and platforms does BlockID support?
BlockID is available on iOS, Android, and as a web application. We also provide APIs for developers to integrate BlockID verification into their own applications. Our platform is designed to work across multiple devices while maintaining the highest security standards.
How secure is the biometric data stored by BlockID?
BlockID never stores your actual biometric data. Instead, we create a mathematical representation (template) of your biometric features that cannot be reverse-engineered to recreate your original biometric data. This template is encrypted and stored securely on your device, ensuring your biometric information remains private and protected.
